About ZenFlow

An AI-powered digital wellness platform built for Gen Z and beyond.

Our Mission

Helping people understand their relationship with technology

Social media is engineered to be addictive. ZenFlow gives you the data and insights to make informed decisions about how you use it — without judgment.

We built a Random Forest machine learning model trained on real-world screen time and wellbeing data. Combined with a rule-based recommendation engine, it generates personalised guidance that goes beyond generic advice.

Everything runs privately. No accounts. No data stored. Your assessment is analysed on the spot and the results are yours alone.
